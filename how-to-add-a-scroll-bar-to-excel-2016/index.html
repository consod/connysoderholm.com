How to add a Scroll Bar to Excel 2016

Excel Conny Söderholm 31.05.2018
(Updated on 03.05.2023 )

Scroll Bar - Do you sometimes change a cell in order to see what a calculated value turns into in Excel? In this post, we are going to cover how to add a scroll bar and link it to a cell. In this way, you can easily try out several combinations of values for a formula.

First, you need to activate your Developer Tab. To do this, navigate to File→Options→Customize Ribbon. To the right, under Main Tabs, check the Developer box. When you have activated the Developer Tab, you can now insert a Scroll Bar. Insert it from Insert→Form Controls→Scroll Bar.

Customize Ribbon Tab

Inserting a Scroll Bar

Right-click on the Scroll Bar and choose Format Control. In the cell link, assign cell A1. You are done! When you click the Scroll Bar, it will update the value in the cell accordingly. PC Tutorials has made a great video on how to create Scroll Bars as well.
